> **AI Foundations** | 6 modules | ~8-12 hours

## Overview

This section is for learners who want a solid understanding of AI without needing an engineering background first.

The purpose is not to make you a model builder. The purpose is to make you a competent, skeptical, productive AI user who understands what these systems are good at, where they fail, and how to work with them responsibly.

## Outcome

By the end of this section, you should be able to:
- explain what AI and LLMs actually are at a useful level
- use prompting as a communication skill, not magic incantation
- verify outputs instead of trusting them blindly
- set basic privacy and safety boundaries
- use AI as a support tool for real work and learning
