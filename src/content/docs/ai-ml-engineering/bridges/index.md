Bridge pages help AI and ML learners move from building individual systems into operating shared infrastructure. Use them when your next step involves platform responsibility, multi-tenant GPU environments, private infrastructure, model serving operations, governance, or production reliability beyond a single application.

| Bridge | Use it when |
|---|---|
| [From AI Builder to AI Platform Engineer](/ai-ml-engineering/bridges/ai-builder-to-platform-engineer/) | You can build AI applications and want to operate shared training, inference, data, and model-serving platforms for other teams. |
| [From Home Lab AI to Private AI Infrastructure](/ai-ml-engineering/bridges/home-lab-to-private-ai-infra/) | You run AI on personal hardware and need to understand enterprise on-premises GPU infrastructure, security, storage, networking, and procurement. |
