Bridge pages help you move from Kubernetes certification knowledge into adjacent operating contexts. Use them when you already know the core Kubernetes mechanics but the next track asks for assumptions, vocabulary, and failure modes that were not covered by CKA, CKAD, or CKS.

| Bridge | Use it when |
|---|---|
| [Are You Ready for On-Prem Kubernetes?](/k8s/bridges/onprem-readiness/) | You can operate Kubernetes in managed environments and need to understand the infrastructure responsibilities that appear on bare metal. |
| [From Cluster Admin to Platform Engineer](/k8s/bridges/cluster-admin-to-platform-engineer/) | You have strong cluster administration skills and want to move into SRE, DevEx, GitOps, and internal platform work. |
