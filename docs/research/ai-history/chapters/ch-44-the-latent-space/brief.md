# Brief: Chapter 44 - The Latent Space

## Thesis
Word2Vec proved that the semantic meaning of human language could be mathematically mapped into high-dimensional continuous vector spaces. This transformed words from discrete symbols into computable distances, providing the foundational representation infrastructure for all subsequent language models.

## Scope
- IN SCOPE: Tomas Mikolov, Word2Vec, continuous bag-of-words (CBOW), skip-gram, vector arithmetic (King - Man + Woman = Queen), the shift from discrete to continuous representations.
- OUT OF SCOPE: Transformers (Part 8).

## Scenes Outline
1. **The Curse of Dimensionality:** Representing a vocabulary of 100,000 words as one-hot discrete vectors is computationally inefficient and lacks semantic understanding.
2. **The Vector Space:** Mikolov and the Google team embed words into a dense, continuous high-dimensional space.
3. **Semantic Arithmetic:** The realization that spatial distance in this latent space perfectly mirrors semantic and syntactic relationships.
