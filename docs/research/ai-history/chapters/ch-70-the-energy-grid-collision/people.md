## Narrative Handling

This is an infrastructure chapter. The protagonists are clocks, wires, and
procurement offices as much as people. Human tension should come from planning
mismatches: a model team wants capacity this decade; a utility sees queues,
permits, turbines, transformers, and ratepayers.
