# Timeline: Chapter 58

- **2015:** Sohl-Dickstein, Weiss, Maheswaranathan, and Ganguli publish "Deep Unsupervised Learning using Nonequilibrium Thermodynamics," framing generative modeling as forward diffusion plus learned reverse diffusion.
- **2020:** Ho, Jain, and Abbeel publish DDPM, reporting high-quality image synthesis and an accessible noise-prediction parameterization.
- **2021:** Dhariwal and Nichol publish "Diffusion Models Beat GANs on Image Synthesis," showing architecture and guidance improvements that surpass GAN baselines on several image metrics/datasets.
- **2021/2022:** Rombach, Blattmann, Lorenz, Esser, and Ommer publish latent diffusion, moving denoising from pixel space into a compressed autoencoder latent space and adding cross-attention conditioning.
- **2022-08-10:** Stability AI announces a researcher release of Stable Diffusion, explicitly tying it to CompVis/Runway latent diffusion, LAION-Aesthetics, consumer-GPU feasibility, and a 4,000 A100 training cluster.
- **2022-08-22:** Stability AI announces the public release of Stable Diffusion, pointing to weights, code, model card, safety classifier, OpenRAIL-M license, and v1.4 memory footprint.
