# Scene Sketches: Chapter 62 - Multimodal Convergence

## Scene 1: Language Learns To Point

- **Action:** Start before chat products. CLIP shows that language can supervise
  visual concepts at web scale; Flamingo then shows interleaved text, image, and
  video sequences as something a model can condition on in few-shot settings.
- **Evidence Layer:** CLIP p.1/p.2; Flamingo p.1 and Section 1 pp.3-4.
- **Narrative Job:** Explain why multimodality was not just "add a camera to a
  chatbot." The research bridge had to make images and words share an interface.
- **Guardrail:** Keep this to a precursor bridge. Do not rewrite Ch58's
  diffusion history or make CLIP the chapter's center of gravity.

## Scene 2: Vision Enters The Chat Window

- **Action:** Move into GPT-4 and GPT-4V. The user can give images, screenshots,
  diagrams, documents, and photos alongside text; the assistant responds in
  text. Be My AI supplies a concrete deployment scene, but the system card also
  shows hallucinations, errors, medical limits, and image-borne jailbreaks.
- **Evidence Layer:** GPT-4 Technical Report p.1 and Section 4.1 p.17; GPT-4V
  System Card pp.1-10.
- **Narrative Job:** Make the interface shift visible: a prompt becomes a mixed
  media bundle rather than a string.
- **Guardrail:** Do not claim GPT-4/GPT-4V generated images, audio, or video.

## Scene 3: Native Multimodality Becomes A Product Claim

- **Action:** Use Gemini to show how labs began selling the model category
  itself as multimodal. The safe claim is joint training across text, image,
  audio, and video plus support for interleaved multimodal input sequences.
- **Evidence Layer:** Gemini report p.1 and architecture section pp.3-4.
- **Narrative Job:** Explain the difference between a text model wrapped in
  peripheral systems and a report that frames multimodality as built into the
  training/interface design.
- **Guardrail:** Attribute "natively multimodal" to the source and define it
  narrowly. Do not generalize it to every product claim.

## Scene 4: Speech Collapses The Interface

- **Action:** GPT-4o makes voice a first-class interface. The contrast is the
  prior three-model Voice Mode pipeline versus one end-to-end model that can
  reason across audio, vision, and text with low audio response latency.
- **Evidence Layer:** GPT-4o System Card p.1; Hello GPT-4o lines 47-48 and
  82-84.
- **Narrative Job:** Show why multimodal convergence changed human-computer
  interaction, not just benchmarks. Latency, turn-taking, emotional tone,
  background sound, and interruption all matter.
- **Guardrail:** Keep output modalities exact: GPT-4o system card says text,
  audio, and image outputs, not video output.

## Scene 5: Video Becomes The Hard Case

- **Action:** Sora and Veo push the category into time. Sora provides the
  strongest technical spine: spacetime patches, diffusion transformers, variable
  duration/resolution/aspect ratio, and explicit simulator limitations. Veo adds
  official competing product framing around 1080p, longer-than-a-minute video.
- **Evidence Layer:** Sora world-simulators lines 33-58 and 118-139; Sora System
  Card lines 51-104; Google Veo blog lines 263-264, 311-322, 393-397.
- **Narrative Job:** Explain why video stresses temporal consistency, safety,
  moderation, and claims about physical understanding.
- **Guardrail:** Do not treat "world simulator" as proven robust world modeling.
  Veo remains Yellow product framing.

## Scene 6: The Category Breaks

- **Action:** Close by naming the category collapse. "LLM" remains common
  shorthand because language is still the command surface, but it no longer
  describes the whole machine: the frontier product reads images, speaks, hears,
  watches, generates media, and routes safety through modality-specific gates.
- **Evidence Layer:** Synthesize only from the already cited sources; no new
  claims.
- **Narrative Job:** Hand off to Ch63, Ch66, and Ch68: the same convergence that
  makes products feel magical also makes inference cost, evaluation politics,
  and data-rights fights harder.
- **Guardrail:** Do not add a new unsupported grand theory about consciousness,
  embodiment, or physical understanding.
