# Timeline: Chapter 64 - The Edge Compute Bottleneck

- **2017-04:** MobileNets frames neural-network architecture as a mobile and
  embedded deployment problem, not just an accuracy contest.
- **2017-09:** Apple announces iPhone X with A11 Bionic Neural Engine and
  on-device Face ID processing.
- **2023-10:** Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 product brief markets phone silicon
  around on-device multimodal generative AI, 10B-parameter support, and NPU
  performance/watt gains.
- **2023-12:** Google ships Gemini Nano on Pixel 8 Pro for Recorder Summarize and
  Smart Reply, while also showing cloud fallback through Video Boost.
- **2023-12:** Apple's LLM-in-a-flash paper studies how to run models larger
  than available DRAM by storing weights in flash and loading them on demand.
- **2024-02 / 2024-06:** MobileLLM develops sub-billion-parameter language
  models for mobile use cases and publishes a June 2024 revision.
- **2024-06:** Apple introduces Apple Intelligence and frames the stack around a
  ~3B on-device language model plus Private Cloud Compute for larger requests.
- **2024-07:** Apple publishes the full Apple Intelligence Foundation Language
  Models report, documenting AFM-on-device architecture, pruning/distillation,
  quantization, and deployment details.
- **2024-10:** Android opens experimental Gemini Nano access through AICore,
  explicitly presenting on-device models as smaller and less generalized than
  cloud equivalents.
