# People: Chapter 28 - The Second AI Winter

- **John McDermott:** CMU researcher associated with XCON/R1 and later defense expert-system technology-transfer work referenced by Atkinson.
- **Gerald L. Atkinson:** Author of the IAAI-90 account of automated knowledge-acquisition tools for defense technology transfer.
- **Navaratna S. Rajaram:** NASA/JSC Summer Faculty author documenting expert systems from a human-factors and technology-transfer perspective.
- **Bernd Neumann:** Author of the 1988 XCON/configuration-system analysis that supplies the chapter's strongest maintenance evidence.
- **Craig Fields:** DARPA deputy/director figure in Strategic Computing's late-1980s politics and survival/reorientation.
- **Jack Schwartz:** DARPA ISTO director whose critique of AI scaling and cuts to AI/expert-system programs are central to the winter scene in Roland/Shiman.
- **Raj Reddy and Allen Newell:** CMU figures appearing in Roland/Shiman's account of the DARPA review pressure around AI programs.
- **Steve Squires:** Architectures/HPC advocate in Roland/Shiman; important for the reallocation from machine intelligence toward high-performance computing.
- **Tohru Moto-oka:** Late professor credited in FGCS proceedings with initiating Japan's FGCS project.
- **Hidehiko Tanaka and Hideaki Kumano:** FGCS'92/MITI foreword figures useful for final-results and official framing of FGCS.
